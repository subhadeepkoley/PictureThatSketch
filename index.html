Subhadeep Koley (শুভদীপ)

I am a Doctor of Philosophy (PhD) student, focusing on Computer Vision and Deep Learning, at SketchX Lab of Centre for Vision, Speech and Signal Processing (CVSSP), University of Surrey, England, United Kingdom. My primary supervisor is Prof. Yi-Zhe Song; co-supervised by Prof. Tao (Tony) Xiang.

Prior to this, I worked as an Image Processing & Deep Learning Engineer at The MathWorks, specializing in Digital Image Processing, Pattern Recognition, Multi & Hyperspectral imaging, Deep Learning, and Medical Image Analysis.

Prior to that, I worked as a Graduate Engineer Trainee at Johnson Controls.

Email  /  CV  /  LinkedIn  /  GitHub  /  Google Scholar  /  ORCiD  /  DBLP

Research Interests

My research focus is broadly centered around Deep Learning and its applications in Computer Vision problems. My specific area of interest is deep learning-based visual understanding of hand-drawn sketches. Our research group aims at understanding "how seeing can be explained by drawing".
